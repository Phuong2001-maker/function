Privacy policy

FluxConvert processes every file inside your browser. We do not upload, view, or store your content.

On-device processing

Converters execute with Web APIs inside the browser tab. There is no server-side queue, so your device power is the only limit.

No uploads or retention

Files never reach our servers. Once you close the tab the working data is cleared automatically.

Contact details

If you email us or submit the contact form we only use that information to reply. We do not share or sell it.
